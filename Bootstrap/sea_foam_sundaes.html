  • Lavendar Honey

    Lavender Honey is made from the nectar of Lavandula stoechas luisieri, which grows wild on the border between Spain and Portugal and blankets the mountainsides in purple flowers during the month of May. Lavender honey is a sought after varietal popular around the world.

    Blueberry Dream

    Blueberry Dream is made with Blueberries, rasberries & dark chocolate. It is garnished with edible moss from the surroundings forest of Portland. The fruit is gathered and grown locally by Oregon Farmers. This ice cream is a taste of the wild & woodsy.

    Chocolate Chip Challenge

    The chocolate chip challenge is so named because of the five different types of chips included: dark, milk, mint, peanut-butter & hazzelnut. The ice cream is prepared and garnished in peanut shells, gathered from Lars' farm located near Forest Grove, OR.
