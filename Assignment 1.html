Bridges of Portland

A collection and brief description of bridges in Portland, Oregon

St. Johns Bridge

The St. Johns Bridge is a steel suspension bridge that spans the Willamette River in Portland, Oregon, United States, between the Cathedral Park neighborhood in North Portland and the Linnton and Northwest Industrial neighborhoods in Northwest Portland. The steel suspension bridge with dual gothic-style towers is the tallest in Portland.

Date

Construction started in September 1929 and was completed in June 1931
